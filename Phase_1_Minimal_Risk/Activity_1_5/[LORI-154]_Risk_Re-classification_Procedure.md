# AI Risk Re-classification Procedure

- **Trigger:** A significant change to the system or its use case.
- **Process:** The system undergoes a full re-classification using the AI Risk Classification Procedure.
- **Documentation:** A new AI Risk Classification Justification Form is completed.
- **Approval:** The AI Governance Body approves the new classification.
