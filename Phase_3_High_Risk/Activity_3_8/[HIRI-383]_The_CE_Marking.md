# The CE Marking

**Source:** Activity_3_8


The CE marking must be affixed visibly, legibly, and indelibly. For digital products, it can be displayed on the "About" screen or during the system's start-up.

![CE Marking](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e7/CE_logo.svg/1200px-CE_logo.svg.png)

*This is the official format of the CE marking. High-resolution vector files can be downloaded from the European Commission's website.*
