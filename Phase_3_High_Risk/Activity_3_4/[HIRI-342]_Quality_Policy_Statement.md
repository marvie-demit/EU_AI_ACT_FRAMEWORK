# [Your Company Name] Quality Policy

At [Your Company Name], our mission is to develop innovative and trustworthy Artificial Intelligence solutions. We are committed to a culture of quality that prioritizes the safety, fundamental rights, and success of our customers.

Our Quality Management System (QMS) is designed to ensure that our High-Risk AI systems consistently meet the highest standards of performance, reliability, and compliance with all applicable legal and regulatory requirements, including the EU AI Act.

We are committed to:

*   **Customer Focus:** Understanding and meeting the needs of our customers.
*   **Compliance:** Adhering to all legal and regulatory requirements for our products.
*   **Continuous Improvement:** Regularly reviewing and improving our processes, products, and our QMS itself.
*   **Risk-Based Thinking:** Integrating risk management into all aspects of our product lifecycle.

This policy is communicated and understood throughout our organization and is reviewed annually for continued suitability.
