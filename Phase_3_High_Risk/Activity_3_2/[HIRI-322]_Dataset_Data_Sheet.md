# Dataset Data Sheet

**Source:** Activity_3_2


*(Inspired by the "Datasheets for Datasets" paper by Gebru et al.)*

```markdown
# Data Sheet for [Dataset Name]

**Version:** [e.g., 2.1]
**Date:** [Date]
**High-Risk AI System(s):** [e.g., Resume Screener / AI-003]

## 1. Motivation & Composition

*   **Purpose:** Why was this dataset created? What is its intended use for the AI system?
*   **Instances:** How many instances (rows) are in the dataset? What does each instance represent?
*   **Features:** What are the features (columns)? Are they numerical, categorical, text, etc.?

## 2. Collection & Provenance

*   **Source:** Where did the data come from (e.g., internal database, public source, third-party provider)?
*   **Collection Method:** How was the data collected (e.g., user submissions, web scraping, sensor data)?
*   **Legal & Ethical Basis:** What is the legal basis for using this data (e.g., user consent under GDPR, legitimate interest)? Was an ethics review conducted?

## 3. Pre-processing & Cleaning

*   **Data Cleaning:** What steps were taken to clean the data (e.g., handling missing values, correcting errors)?
*   **Data Labeling:** How was the data labeled? Was it done manually or automatically? What quality controls were in place?

## 4. Bias & Fairness Assessment

*   **Demographic Representation:** Does the dataset contain demographic information (e.g., age, gender, ethnicity)? If so, what is the distribution? Are any groups underrepresented?
*   **Known Biases & Limitations:** What potential sources of bias have been identified (e.g., selection bias, historical bias)? What are the known limitations of this dataset?
*   **Bias Mitigation Steps:** What steps, if any, were taken to mitigate the identified biases (e.g., re-sampling, data augmentation)?

## 5. Distribution & Maintenance

*   **Update Cadence:** How often is this dataset updated?
*   **Maintenance:** Who is responsible for maintaining this dataset?


**Completed by:** [Name, Role]
**Reviewed by (Data Science Lead):** [Name]
**Approved by (AI Governance Body):** [Name]
**Date of Approval:** [Date]
```
