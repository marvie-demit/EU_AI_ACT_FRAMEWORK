# Log Retention Policy for High-Risk AI Systems

- **Scope:** Applies to all logs generated by High-Risk AI systems.
- **Retention Period:** All logs must be retained for a minimum of **10 years**.
- **Storage:** Logs must be stored in a secure, immutable, and tamper-proof manner.
- **Access:** Access is restricted to authorized personnel for audit and investigation purposes.
