# Change Management Procedure for High-Risk AI Systems

**Purpose:** To ensure all changes to High-Risk AI systems are reviewed, tested, and approved before deployment.

1. **Change Request:** All changes must be initiated via a Change Request Form.
2. **Impact Assessment:** The AI Governance Body assesses the impact on risk, performance, and compliance.
3. **Testing:** The change undergoes full regression testing.
4. **Approval:** The AI Governance Body approves the change.
5. **Deployment:** The change is deployed to production.
6. **Documentation:** All documentation is updated.
