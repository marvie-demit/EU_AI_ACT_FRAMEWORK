**Effective Date:** [Date]

[Your Company's Full Legal Name]

(Hereinafter referred to as "the Provider")

**And**

**The Authorized Representative:**
[Representative's Full Legal Name]
[Representative's Registered Address, within the EU]
(Hereinafter referred to as "the Representative")

## 1. Appointment

The Provider hereby appoints the Representative, and the Representative hereby accepts the appointment, to act as the Provider's authorized representative within the European Union for the purposes of complying with the obligations set out in Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (the Artificial Intelligence Act).

## 2. Tasks of the Representative (as per Article 55(3))

The Representative is mandated to perform the following tasks on behalf of the Provider:

*   **a)** Verify that the technical documentation referred to in Article 53 has been drawn up and that the provider has complied with the obligations set out in Article 53(1)(d) and (e).
*   **b)** Keep a copy of the technical documentation at the disposal of the AI Office and national competent authorities for a period of 10 years after the GPAI model has been placed on the market.
*   **c)** Provide the AI Office and national competent authorities, upon a reasoned request, with all the information and documentation necessary to demonstrate compliance.
*   **d)** Cooperate with the AI Office and national competent authorities, upon a reasoned request, on any action they take in relation to the GPAI model.

## 3. Liability

The Representative shall be liable for any damage caused by the GPAI model in the event of its non-compliance with the AI Act, without prejudice to the liability of the Provider.

## 4. Termination

This mandate shall remain in effect until terminated by either party with [e.g., 90 days] written notice. The Provider is obligated to appoint a new representative before the termination becomes effective.
