## üéØ Templates by Risk Level

Start here regardless of your system's risk level:
- Phase 1: Activities 1.1 through 1.5 (Governance & Classification)

### **Limited Risk AI Systems**
Chatbots, deepfakes, emotion recognition:
- Phase 2: All activities (Transparency obligations)

### **High-Risk AI Systems**

- Phase 3: All activities (Comprehensive compliance)

Foundation models, LLMs:
- GPAI Parallel Track: All activities (Model-level obligations)

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Start with Phase 1** - Even if you have high-risk systems, complete Phase 1 first
2. **Customize, don't copy** - Replace all [Your Company Name] placeholders
3. **Get legal review** - Have your legal team review policies before approval
4. **Version control** - Maintain version history for all completed templates
5. **Link documents** - Cross-reference related templates for audit trail
