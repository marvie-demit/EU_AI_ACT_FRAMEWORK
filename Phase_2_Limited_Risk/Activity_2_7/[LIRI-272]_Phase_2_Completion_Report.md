## 1. Executive Summary

This report confirms the successful completion of Phase 2 of the EU AI Act Stepwise Implementation Plan. All AI systems classified as "Limited Risk" have had their mandatory transparency obligations under Article 52 of the Act implemented, verified, and documented.

## 2. Scope of Work

This phase addressed three categories of transparency obligations:

*   **User Interaction Notifications (Article 52(1)):** For systems like chatbots.
*   **Content Generation Labeling (Article 52(3)):** For systems that create "deepfakes" or synthetic media.
*   **Biometric/Emotion System Disclosures (Article 52(2)):** For systems processing sensitive biometric or emotion data.
