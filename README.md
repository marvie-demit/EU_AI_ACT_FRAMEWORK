# EU AI Act Compliance Framework

**A Complete Collection of Production-Ready Templates and Implementation Guides for EU AI Act Compliance**

---

## Overview

The EU AI Act Compliance Framework provides a comprehensive, stepwise implementation approach for organizations seeking compliance with Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act). This framework contains production-ready templates, detailed activity guides, and supporting materials organized by risk level and implementation phase.

**Framework Contents:**
- **118 documents** including activity guides, templates, and master references
- **Comprehensive ID system** for easy navigation and traceability
- **Author attribution** on all documents (Dipl. Ing. Marvie Demit)
- **Progressive compliance approach** from Minimal Risk to High-Risk AI systems
- **Parallel GPAI track** for General Purpose AI model providers
- **Enhanced activity guides** with practical implementation tasks, checklists, and pitfalls
- **Separate templates** to eliminate duplication and enable reuse

All materials are organized by implementation phase and activity, following the risk-based approach mandated by the EU AI Act.

## How to Use This Framework

### **Step 1: Understand the Framework**
Read `[FRMW-005] EU_AI_Act_Stepwise_Implementation_Guide_Final_v2.md` to understand the overall structure and risk-based approach.

### **Step 2: Identify Your Implementation Path**
Consult `[FRMW-002] QUICK_REFERENCE.md` to determine which phases apply to your organization based on your AI systems' risk levels.

### **Step 3: Follow Activity Guides Sequentially**
For each activity:
1. Open the `[ID]_Activity_Complete_Guide.md` file
2. Review the purpose, deliverables, and implementation tasks
3. Identify and customize the related templates (separate files in the same folder)
4. Complete the activity before moving to the next

### **Step 4: Maintain Documentation**
- Keep completed templates in version control
- Link related documents for audit trail
- Update templates when systems or processes change
